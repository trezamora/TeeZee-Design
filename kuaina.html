Kuaina the Podcast

I was tasked to develop, create, and design the visual identity for Kuaina, a small locally managed and owned brand that promotes the empowerment of small businesses and entrepreneurs in Hawai ªi.

Process & Insight

Threading Business & Culture

Kuaina is the embodiment of love for the land and culture an endless cycle of respect, love, community, and aloha. Community plays a big part in how we connect, learn and grow. Kuaina goal is to share the stories of hardwork business owners and entrepreneurs.

Solution

Grounded & Centered

Earthy tones, community, and heartwarming stories. Kuaina is at the center of stringing together Hawaiian culture and Small businesses and entrepreneurs.
