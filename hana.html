Hana, Building Bridges

I was tasked to create Instagram carousel post for the Kapiʻolani Community College STEM program that highlighted and reflected Native Hawaiian culture. Each post pairs a modern STEM career with a traditional practice or methedology from Hawaiʻi's history.

Process & Insight

Bridging Culture and Design

The bridge created started with research of STEM careers and Native Hawaiian culture. An illustration style was selected based on target audience.

Solution

A Framework to share

Through this process a framework was created that resulted in the incorporation of STEM, Culture, and Design. With this framework, careful consideration on how design can be used as a tool to educate and represent cultures in a respectful and beautiful manner.
